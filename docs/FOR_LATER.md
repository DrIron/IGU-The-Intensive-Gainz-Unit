## Testimonials & coach reputation

> **PLANNED (2026-07-05) — see `docs/COACH_PROFILE_TESTIMONIALS_PLAN.md`** (full build spec). Combines the
> whole arc below **+ the per-coach public profile page (`/coach/:slug`)** into one plan. Key decisions:
> curation is **admin-driven** (admin picks what shows on public pages + coach pages; not coach
> self-curation); testimonials gain optional **client-attached proof** (avg weight change first, then a
> lift-progression pick-a-lift attachment + note); reputation lives on the coach's public page. Foundation
> = the clients-only submission gate (`docs/TESTIMONIALS_CLIENTS_ONLY_BUILD.md`). Phased T0–T4. The notes
> below are the original parked context.

**Current state (2026-07-04):**
- Testimonials are submitted at `/testimonial` (a form, now gated to real clients of the reviewed coach — see `docs/TESTIMONIALS_CLIENTS_ONLY_BUILD.md`).
- Approved testimonials display **only** as a section on the Index landing page ("What Our Clients Say"). No dedicated view page, no filtering.
- The public "Testimonials" nav item points at the submit form (not a view) — so non-clients hit the "clients only" state. **This is the near-term thing to fix** (see "Near-term" below).
- Coach profiles and Meet Our Team do **not** show testimonials.

**Deferred ideas:**

1. **Filter public testimonials by coach.** On a dedicated public testimonials view page, let visitors filter/sort by coach (and maybe by goal/result). Needs: a coach filter control + the view page (see Near-term item 1 as the prerequisite). Small-to-medium once the view page exists.

2. **Coach-curated testimonials on their profile.** Let a coach choose which of *their* approved testimonials appear on their coach-facing profile surfaces. Approach: add a coach-controlled `featured_on_profile` (or `coach_visible`) boolean on `testimonials` (coach can toggle only rows where `coach_id = auth.uid()`, and only already-`is_approved` rows — admin approval still gates what's eligible). New coach-facing "My testimonials" curation UI (list their approved testimonials, toggle which are featured). Touches: `testimonials` schema + RLS (coach UPDATE of the flag on own-coach rows), a new coach settings/profile tab.

3. **Show curated testimonials on Meet Our Team + coach profile.** Render each coach's `featured_on_profile` testimonials on their card/expanded profile (`MeetOurTeam.tsx`, `CoachDetailDialog.tsx`, `CoachProfile.tsx`). Depends on #2.

4. **Individual coach public profile pages.** e.g. `/coach/:slug` — a richer public page per coach (bio, specialties, certs, their curated testimonials, CTA). Would make #1/#3 land somewhere coherent. Larger; also intersects onboarding coach-selection (ON2 on the design board).

**Rough sequencing when we pick this up:** Near-term view/submit split → filter-by-coach → coach curation flag + UI → render on team/profile → (optionally) per-coach public pages.
