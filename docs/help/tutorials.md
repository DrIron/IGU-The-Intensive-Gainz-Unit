# Step-by-step tutorials

A complete how-to for every feature on IGU, written for end users. Each entry has a short **description** and numbered steps. Tier/level notes mark anything that differs by plan (client) or by level / subrole / head-coach status (coach).

`##` = a section (a group in the Tutorials menu); `###` = one feature tutorial.

- **Part A — Client tutorials** (plans: Team Plan · 1:1 Online · Hybrid · In-Person)
- **Part B — Coach tutorials** (general coach, dietitian and other specialists, levels, head coach)

> Current model: four plans (1:1 Complete retired). Nutrition (dietitian) and physiotherapy are add-ons **launching soon**.

---

# Part A — Client tutorials

## A1. Getting started

### Signing up & completing the intake form
A short wizard that joins you to IGU; collects your details, goals, and plan, and saves progress so you can finish later.

1. Go to theigu.com and choose a plan, or open your sign-up link.
2. Step 1 — your details, goals, and plan. (1:1 plans let you name a preferred coach.)
3. Step 2 — plan-specific questions (team acknowledgements, or 1:1 experience/days/gym access).
4. Step 3 — the seven health (PAR-Q) questions.
5. Step 4 — accept the legal agreements.
6. Submit Application, or Save & Exit to finish later.

### The health check (PAR-Q)
Seven health-readiness questions to confirm training is safe before you start.

1. Answer each truthfully; add injuries/conditions in the notes if relevant.
2. Submit — if anything is flagged, your application goes to a brief medical review (24–48h).

> Your medical answers are private and encrypted; your coach only sees whether you’re cleared to train.

### Activating your account
The final step that switches your account on after your coach approves you.

1. On the activation screen, review your plan and price.
2. Enter a promo code if you have one.
3. Complete the secure checkout — your dashboard unlocks when confirmed.

> **Pricing:** Team Plan is a flat 10 KWD/month; the 1:1 plans are priced by your coach’s level (Online from 30, Hybrid from 95, In-Person from 145 KWD). You always see your price before you confirm.

### Choosing your team — *Team Plan only*
1. On your dashboard, open the **Choose your team** prompt.
2. Review the available teams and spaces.
3. Select a team and confirm **Join Team**.

### Changing your team — *Team Plan only*
1. Open your team card and choose **Change team**.
2. Pick a new team and confirm. (Limited to about once per billing cycle.)

## A2. Your dashboard

### Reading your dashboard
Home base — today’s workout, nutrition targets, weekly progress, coach, billing, alerts.

1. Log in to land on your dashboard.
2. Scan top to bottom; tap any card to open that area.

### Logging weight and steps (Log Today)
1. Find the **Log Today** card.
2. Enter today’s weight (kg) and steps, and save (aim for three weigh-ins a week).

### Requesting a deload
1. Tap **Request a deload**, add an optional note, and submit.
2. Your coach reviews it; if declined you can ask again after a short cool-off.

### Acting on alerts & reminders
1. Read any banner at the top of the dashboard.
2. Tap its action (e.g. **Renew**) to resolve it.

## A3. Workouts

### Starting and logging a workout
1. From your dashboard, tap **Start** on today’s workout.
2. For each exercise, check the target and watch the video if needed.
3. Enter weight, reps, and effort per set, then tick it off (saves instantly).
4. Use the rest timer between sets.
5. Tap **Complete Workout** when done.

### Using the rest timer
1. Tick a set as done — the timer starts.
2. Pause or skip; start your next set when it ends.

### Swapping an exercise
1. Open the exercise menu → **Swap**.
2. Pick an alternative and log it; message your coach so they can update your plan.

### Viewing your workout calendar
1. Open **Calendar**; move between months and read the colour key.
2. Tap a day to see and open its sessions.

### Reviewing your exercise history
1. Open **Exercise History**, select a logged exercise.
2. Review the trend and the set-by-set table.

### Browsing the exercise library
1. Open the **Exercise Library**, search or filter by muscle.
2. Open an exercise for video, setup, execution, and pitfalls.

## A4. Nutrition — 1:1 plans (Online, Hybrid, In-Person)
Your coach sets and adjusts your targets; you log data and do a weekly check-in. *(A dietitian add-on that leads your nutrition is launching soon.)*

### Understanding your nutrition phase
1. Open **Nutrition** and read the phase card (calories, macros, goal, on-track status).

### Logging your weight
1. In Nutrition → **This Week** (or Log Today), enter your weight and save (3+/week).

### Logging steps / measurements / body fat
1. In **This Week**, open the relevant card (steps, measurements, or body fat).
2. Enter the values and save.

### Completing your weekly check-in
1. Log at least three weigh-ins that week to unlock it.
2. Answer the questions (calories hit, changes, tracking accuracy) and add notes.
3. Submit — your coach reviews and may adjust.

### Viewing your nutrition history
1. Open the **History** tab to see weight and body-fat trends.

## A5. Nutrition — Team Plan
You manage your own nutrition with a guided calculator.

### Setting your own nutrition goal
1. Open **Nutrition → Goal Setting**.
2. Work through the wizard (stats, activity, goal, rate).
3. Review the calories/macros and **Save**.

### Tracking your progress
1. Open the **Progress Tracker** tab.
2. Expand each week to log data and view graphs.

## A6. Education

### Watching educational videos
1. Open **Educational Videos**; browse sections or search.
2. Watch in the secure player; tap **Mark as Complete**.

### Following a learning path
1. Open **Learning Paths**, start a path, work through it in order.

### Watching content your coach assigned
1. Look for “required” / “from your coach” highlights.
2. Open and complete them — your coach sees your progress.

## A7. Sessions
*(On plans that include session booking.)*

### Booking a 1:1 session
1. Open **Sessions** and check your weekly usage.
2. Pick a date, choose a slot, and confirm.

### Cancelling a session
1. In **Upcoming**, choose **Cancel** and confirm.

> During a grace period you keep view access, but new bookings pause until you’re paid up.

## A8. Messages

### Messaging your care team
1. Open **Messages**, type, and send (Cmd/Ctrl+Enter on desktop).
2. Your coach replies in the same thread.

### Editing or deleting your message
1. Open the menu on your own message → **Edit** or **Delete**.

## A9. Add-ons

### Browsing and adding an add-on
1. Open **Add-on Services**; filter by type.
2. Open an add-on to see what’s included and eligibility.
3. Choose it, set quantity, and check out securely.

> **Coming soon:** nutrition (dietitian) and physiotherapy add-ons. Some specialist add-ons are limited to certain plans.

### Ending a recurring add-on
1. Open your care-team card → on the add-on, choose **End at renewal** and confirm.

## A10. Billing & account

### Renewing your subscription
1. Open **Billing** (or tap a reminder), review your plan, tap **Renew**, and check out.

### Updating your profile
1. Open **Account**, edit your details, and save (email changes need confirmation).

### Changing your password
1. In **Account → password**, enter a new password and save.

### Requesting a coach change
1. In **Account → coach**, choose **Request coach change**, pick a preference, submit.

### Cancelling your subscription
1. In **Account/Billing → subscription**, choose **Cancel** and confirm (ends at period end).

### Deleting your account
1. In **Account → Danger Zone**, choose **Delete account** and confirm.

### Using the calorie calculator
1. Open the **Calorie Calculator**; use the Initial or Adjustment calculator.
2. Review the result (a tool only — it doesn’t save).

# Part B — Coach tutorials

> **Levels & subroles.** Some actions depend on your level (Junior / Senior / Lead), your subrole (Coach, Dietitian, Physiotherapist, Sports Psychologist, Mobility Coach), or being a Head Coach. Dietitian and physio specifics are in B13–B14. Specialist add-ons (nutrition/physio) are launching soon.

## B1. Getting set up

### Completing your coach profile
1. Open **My Profile**; write your bio, add qualifications, pick specialization tags.
2. Add your photo and contacts; save (awaits admin approval).

### Completing training mode
1. From your dashboard, open **Training**.
2. Watch each required video, tick the acknowledgement, **Mark as Complete**.
3. Finishing all required videos activates your account.

## B2. Your dashboard

### Reading your dashboard
1. Start with the needs-attention banner and today’s tasks; scan the activity feed.

### Checking your capacity
1. Open the capacity card to see spots-left / at-capacity per plan type.

### Checking your earnings
1. Read the month-to-date compensation summary on your dashboard.
2. Open the **Payouts** tab in My Clients for the per-client breakdown. (See B12 for how pay works.)

## B3. Client directory & approvals

### Approving or declining a new client
1. Open **My Clients → Pending Approvals**.
2. Review the client; choose **Approve** (moves them to the next step + emails them) or **Decline**.

### Searching and filtering your roster
1. Use the search box and plan filter; watch for unread-message and deload badges.

### Inviting a client — *Senior / Lead only*
1. **My Clients → Invite Client**; enter details and plan; send.

### Adding an exempt client — *Head Coach only*
1. **My Clients → Add Exempt Client**; complete details and confirm.

### Opening a client's overview
1. Click any client row to open their **Client Overview** (tabs in B4).

## B4. The client overview workspace
*(Eight tabs; six for dietitians.)*

- **Overview** — phase, last workout, last weigh-in, pending-adjustment nudge, deload requests.
- **Progress** — nutrition graphs + training volume.
- **Workouts** — programs, day/module drill-down, session logs, adherence.
- **Sessions** — direct sessions + add-on packs; primary coach/admin can schedule.
- **Messages** — the coach↔client thread.
- **Care Team** — roster + staff-only chat; add/discharge specialists (primary coach/admin).
- **Profile** — read-only demographics, subscription, onboarding summary.

(Each tab: open the client, select the tab, act on what it shows.)

## B5. Building programs
*(Coach, physiotherapist, mobility coach.)*

### Calendar builder
1. Open **Program Library**, create or open a program.
2. On a day, **+ Add session** (title, type, timing); add exercises and prescriptions.
3. Use the session menu to edit/copy/publish/delete.

### Copying a week
1. **Copy Week**, pick source and target weeks, confirm.

### Publishing sessions
1. Publish each session, or **Publish All** — only published sessions deliver.

### Planning Board (muscle builder)
1. Open the **Planning Board** and create a plan.
2. Add a session to a day; use the inline **+** picker to place muscles/activities.
3. Tune sets/reps/tempo/effort; edit individual sets.
4. Use week tabs for multiple weeks; watch volume/frequency/progression.
5. Optionally add a deload week, save a preset, attach videos.

### Converting a plan to a program
1. In the Planning Board, choose **Create Program** and confirm.

### Assigning a program
1. From the program, **Assign**; pick a client (or team) and start date. *(Head coaches: team assignment fans out to all active members.)*

### Chaining macrocycles
1. Open **Macrocycles**, create one, add programs in sequence.

## B6. Nutrition coaching
*(Read-only if a dietitian leads that client.)*

### Setting a nutrition phase
1. Client **Nutrition → Overview**; adjust pre-filled demographics, set goal type and rate, save.

### Making weekly adjustments
1. **Adjustments** tab; on a week card choose Increase/Decrease, set amount and notes, apply.

### Scheduling a diet break or refeed
1. In **Adjustments**, use Diet break / Refeed, set dates, confirm.

### Approving/rejecting a client request
1. In **Adjustments**, open the pending request; Approve or Reject.

### Graphs and notes
1. **History** tab; review graphs and add notes.

## B7. Sessions & availability

### Publishing availability
1. **Sessions → Manage Time Slots**; add a slot (date, time, duration, count, type, location); toggle available/blocked.

### Managing bookings
1. **Sessions → Upcoming Sessions**; mark Complete or Cancel.

## B8. Teams — *Head Coach only*

### Creating a team
1. **My Teams → Create Team**; set name, tags, max members, description; save.

### Assigning/changing a team program
1. Open team detail → **Assign Program** (or Change), preview, confirm.

### Managing members / deleting a team
1. Review the member list in team detail.
2. **Delete** + confirm to remove a team (members are detached for reassignment).

## B9. Content assignment

### Assigning content
1. From **Educational Videos**, open a video → **Assign to client**; pick client and due date.

### Tracking assignments
1. Open **Assigned Content**; review status/due/completion; remove if needed.

## B10. Care team & specialists

### Adding a specialist — *Primary coach / admin*
1. Client **Care Team → Add specialist**; search by specialty, set scope (full/view-only), confirm.

### Discharging a specialist
1. **Care Team → Discharge** on the specialist, confirm.

### Managing your own assignments
1. Open **My Assignments**; review specialty/scope; **End at renewal** to step off.

## B11. Deload requests

### Responding to a deload request
1. Client **Overview** tab → deload panel.
2. **Approve** (preset, optional week, message) or **Decline** (reason; client gets a cool-off).

## B12. Your compensation
You earn a share of each client’s monthly price; on the 1:1 plans your pay rises with your level.

- **Per client / month:** Online 17/24/30 · Hybrid 70/88/105 · In-Person 107/141/183 KWD (Junior/Senior/Lead).
- **Team Plan:** head coach paid a flat **6 KWD** per member/month.
- Levelling up raises both the client price and your pay. Promotions are based on certifications, tenure, retention, and results.
- Guardrails: a minimum IGU margin per subscription and a 30% discount cap.
- *Coming soon:* retention/referral/head-coach upside, and pay for the nutrition/physio add-ons (planned ~21 / ~33 KWD per client/month), confirmed at launch.

## B13. Exercise library & profile

### Browsing exercises
1. Open **Exercise Library**; search/filter; preview videos.

### Editing your profile
1. Open **My Profile**; update photo, bio, qualifications, specialisms, contacts, socials; save.

## B14. Dietitian & specialist differences

### Dietitian
- Nutrition-focused dashboard + a dedicated **Nutrition clients** roster.
- Client overview = six tabs (no Workouts/Sessions), defaults to Nutrition.
- When assigned, you **lead nutrition** and the general coach becomes read-only there.
- You can’t build/assign programs.
- You’re added to a client via the **nutrition add-on** (launching soon).

### Physiotherapist
- Care-team specialist: builds/assigns rehab programs (full scope) and records injury-assessment notes.
- Added via the client’s **Care Team** tab with full or view-only scope; clients appear in **My Assignments**.
- Joins via the **physio add-on** (launching soon).

### Sports psychologist / mobility coach
- Sports psychologist: mindset/performance + psych notes; doesn’t build programs.
- Mobility coach: builds/assigns programs like a coach.
- A coach can hold several credentials at once; capabilities combine.
